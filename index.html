About This App

This advanced habit tracker helps you monitor daily habits, track completion statistics, and write diary notes. Features include:

  • Add, edit, and remove habits effortlessly
  • Mark habits as completed and track progress
  • View detailed statistics with completion percentages
  • Daily diary with persistent storage and easy retrieval
  • Beautiful and modern UI with cards, backgrounds, and animations
  • Optimized for mobile and desktop screens
  • Encourages consistency and personal growth

Whether you're forming new habits or journaling your thoughts, this app is your companion for a productive and mindful life.
